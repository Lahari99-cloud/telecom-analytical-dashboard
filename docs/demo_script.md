# Demo Script

1. Open the dashboard and show KPI cards.
2. Explain that the system is no-LLM and database-backed.
3. Click **Show churn by region**.
4. Show the generated chart, insight, cause, recommendation, impact, and confidence score.
5. Click **Risk vs revenue scatter** to show decision intelligence beyond static BI.
6. Open the chatbot and ask: `Revenue by segment`.
7. Upload a CSV and explain that the database updates without code changes.
8. Show the Region Intelligence View as the telecom geo layer.

Pitch:

> This prototype converts telecom business questions into governed SQL analytics, then generates the right visualization and decision recommendation for customer, product, revenue, churn, and regional operations.
